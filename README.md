# onlineretaildashboard
Online Retail Analysis Dashboard
Project Title / Headline 
🛒 Online Retail Analysis Insights Dashboard An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze online retail performance by tracking revenue trends, customer contribution, product demand across countries, and sales patterns over time.

Short Description / Purpose

The Online Retail Analysis Dashboard is a dynamic Power BI report built to analyze sales performance of an online retail business. It helps identify revenue trends, high-performing countries (excluding UK), top customers, and global product demand patterns to support better business decisions.

Tech Stack 
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

• 📊 Power BI Desktop – Used for designing and developing the complete dashboard report.

• 📂 Power Query – Used for data cleaning, transformation, removing null values, and formatting columns.

• 🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) –Used to create calculated measures such as Revenue, Quantity Sold, and other aggregations.

• 📝 Data Modeling – Built relationships between tables and structured the dataset for correct filtering and analysis.

• 📁 File Format – .pbix (Power BI report file) and .png (dashboard preview screenshots).

Data Source

📌 Dataset Name: Online Retail Dataset

📌 Source Type: Transactional retail sales dataset (Excel/CSV format)

The dataset contains detailed transaction-level information including: • Invoice Number • Stock Code • Product Description • Quantity • Invoice Date • Unit Price • Customer ID • Country This dataset enables analysis of customer purchasing behavior, revenue patterns, and product demand across different regions.

• Business Problem 

Online retail businesses deal with huge amounts of transactional data daily. Without visualization, it becomes difficult to answer important business questions like: Which countries generate the highest sales revenue? What are the monthly and daily revenue trends? Who are the top revenue-generating customers? Which regions show high product demand? How does performance change over time?

• Goal of the Dashboard 

The goal of this dashboard is to provide a single interactive platform that helps stakeholders: Track revenue patterns over time Identify top countries contributing to business growth Analyze customer contribution to revenue Understand global product demand distribution Support expansion strategies outside the UK market

• Walkthrough of Key Visuals

Slicers (Left Panel) 
• Country Slicer: Filters the complete dashboard by selected country. 

• Invoice Date Slicer: Allows filtering between a selected date range for time-based analysis.

Revenue Trend Over Time (Line Chart) 
📌 Count of Revenue by Year, Month, and Day 

• Shows how revenue fluctuates over time. 
• Helps identify peak sales periods and seasonal demand trends.

Top 10 Countries by Revenue and Quantity (Bar Chart) 
📌 Top 10 Countries by Revenue and Quantity (Excluding UK) 
• Compares countries based on total revenue and quantity sold. 
• Useful for identifying potential international markets for business expansion.

Top Customers by Revenue (Horizontal Bar Chart) 
📌 Sum of Revenue by CustomerID 
• Highlights the customers generating the highest revenue. 
• Helps the business identify high-value customers for loyalty programs.

Product Demand by Country (Map Visualization) 
📌 Product Demand by Country (Quantity Sold, Excluding UK) 
• Displays quantity sold distribution across the world using a bubble map.
• Helps visualize international demand and strong-performing regions.

• Business Impact & Insights 
📌 Key Insights from the Dashboard: 
• Certain European countries contribute significantly to sales outside the UK. 
• Revenue trend chart helps detect sales peaks during specific months. 
• A small group of customers contributes a large share of revenue, indicating the importance of customer retention. 
• Map visualization clearly shows strong demand concentrated in Europe, supporting global expansion strategy. 

📌 Business Benefits: 
• Expansion Planning: Identify top international countries for business growth. 
• Customer Strategy: Recognize high-value customers for targeted offers.
• Demand Analysis: Understand global demand distribution to optimize supply chain. 
• Sales Forecasting: Revenue trend patterns help anticipate future high-sales periods.
